Veganism is a philosophy and way of living which seeks to exclude—as far as is possible and practicable—all forms of exploitation of, and cruelty to, animals for food, clothing, or any other purpose. It involves abstaining from animal products in diet (like meat, dairy, eggs, honey) and lifestyle (e.g., leather, wool, cosmetics tested on animals).

A vegan diet focuses on plants: fruits, vegetables, grains, nuts, seeds, and plant-based alternatives.
