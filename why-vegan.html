Going vegan benefits animals by reducing exploitation and suffering, improves health through nutrient-rich plant foods, and helps the environment by lowering emissions and resource use.

Ethically, it aligns with compassion for all sentient beings. Health-wise, it can lower risks of heart disease and diabetes. Environmentally, see our climate page for details.
