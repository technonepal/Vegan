Plant-based proteins include lentils, beans, tofu, tempeh, seitan, nuts, seeds, and quinoa. Meat alternatives like jackfruit, mushrooms, and legume-based burgers provide texture and flavor.

Dairy subs: almond/oat milk, cashew cheese, coconut yogurt. Egg replacers: aquafaba, flaxseeds. These sources are nutritious and sustainable.
